Shell脚本编写

1.将/usr/local/apr目录下大于100k的文件转移到/tmp目录下：

#!/bin/bash
for FileName in ls -l /usr/local/apr | awk '$5>102400' {print $9}
do
mv $FileName /tmp/
done
ls -al /tmp/
echo “done!”
